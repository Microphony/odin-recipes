Pan Mee Recipe

Description

Banmian or pan mee is a popular Chinese noodle dish, consisting of handmade noodles served in soup.

The name banmian (board/block noodle) came from the Hakka method of cutting the noodle into straight strands using a wooden block as ruler. In Hak, some might call it Man-Foon-Char-Guo (麵粉茶粿) or Dao-Ma-Chet (刀嬤切).

Ingredients

  • Hand-torn noodles
  • Fried anchovies
  • Broth
  • Ground pork
  • Vegetables

Directions

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the broth.
  3. Cook the meat and toppings.
  4. Cook the noodles.
  5. Serve.
